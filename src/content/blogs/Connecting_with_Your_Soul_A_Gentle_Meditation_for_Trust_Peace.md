## Why This Meditation Taps Into Your Deepest Wisdom

It is not just a simple relaxation, but a practice that will take you to a deeper level of self-awareness and an ultimate level of safety. With the help of major anchors, such as breath, heart, and strong images of nature, you soon reach the healing inner condition.

### The Mechanism of Connection

The advice is based on several effective strategies that have been borrowed from wellness psychology:

*   **Parasympathetic Activation:** The first thing in this session is to start breathing slower and deeper on purpose. This slow and conscious breathing sends a direct message to your nervous system (through the Vagus nerve) to switch out of fight or flight and switch into rest and digest. This leads to the development of unnecessary muscles, which become slack and soft, and this allows the deep physical relaxation.
*   **Heart-Centered Focus:** You are instructed to concentrate on a loving vibration that is gentle in the heart center. This heart emphasis plays the role of creating coherence, a state of harmony between your heart, brain, and nervous system that has been proven to decrease stress and increase the levels of intuitive clarity.
*   **Oceanic Imagery:** The main visual scene of **"floating on a gentle sea"** with the stars on a blanket is very symbolic. The sea symbolizes the enormousness, profundity, intuition, and creation. The fact that you are held by its beautiful waters makes it true that you are no longer in danger, to relax and be given the nurture and nourishment of the universe.

Such a combination helps you to acknowledge that you are so much more than anything you see and whatever you are facing right now- you are tied to a non-physical self that is immortal and non-physical and which is naturally wise and adored.

2.  **Use Headphones:** It is highly recommended to have headphones. This enhances the soft beat of the music, and it entirely transports you to the bright imagery of the ocean depths and the universe.
3.  **Palms Down (Optional but Recommended):** You are welcome to place your palms downwards. This is one of the poses that is utilized by several traditions to facilitate grounding and assist in bringing your energy and focus back into you.
4.  **Allow Yourself to Be Cradled:** This is a technique of letting go. Relax any muscle tension that you have consciously. You must be able to experience that amazing feeling of being held--by that imaginary sea, and by the love which belongs to your immortal soul.
