> "Begin by getting as comfortable as you'd like right now... Pause if you need to to make sure you can fall asleep during this practice. Coming back once you're cozy, eyes lovingly closed, take a moment to get even more comfortable right now, making any extra subtle adjustments that you need."

As your eyes softly close and your belly becomes *soft and loose*, you invite your breath to be *easy and gentle*. In this state of serene surrender, there’s nothing for you to hold up. You are perfectly safe to allow all your muscles to be *slack and loose*, held completely by your bed.
